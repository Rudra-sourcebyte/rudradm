This model uses a flexible screen, the main screen is soft, the product has been affixed with a special protective film with high adhesion when it leaves the factory, and professional tools are needed to remove and replace it. If you need to replace the main screen protective film, please go to the official customer service center to deal with it. Please do not remove or paste other protective film by yourself, otherwise, it may cause damage to the screen, and the resulting damage to the screen is not covered by the warranty.
